FAQs

Have Questions? Get Answers.

Most homeowners search for answers because something feels off — hot rooms, rising bills, strange odors, or signs of rodents. Here's what we see every day in attics across Southern California.

Attic Shield specializes in attic insulation installation and removal, full rodent proofing with sanitation, air duct replacement, crawl space cleanup & encapsulation, attic fans, and air sealing for energy efficiency. We offer comprehensive attic solutions designed to improve your comfort, safety, and home value.
Old, damaged, or insufficient insulation leads to high energy bills, uncomfortable indoor temperatures, and poor air quality. Upgrading your attic insulation improves your home's energy efficiency, increases comfort year-round, and may qualify you for rebates or federal tax credits like the 25C Energy Efficient Home Improvement Credit.
Our rodent proofing includes a full inspection of your attic and exterior entry points, sealing with galvanized steel mesh, flashing, and permanent materials — no foam, ever. We offer an optional 1-year warranty covering all work performed. Our goal is to stop rodents from entering — permanently.
Yes. We provide a 1-year warranty on rodent proofing for the specific areas we serviced. Our insulation materials come with manufacturer warranties (typically 10–25 years), and we stand behind our labor with a quality guarantee. If any issues arise, we'll make it right.
Absolutely. Attic Shield is fully licensed in California with a C-2 Insulation Contractor License (#1127450) — the only classification legally allowed to perform insulation work under the CSLB. We are also bonded and insured for your protection. Many competitors only hold general licenses or no license at all — we do it by the book.
We proudly serve all of San Diego County (San Diego, Escondido, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Vista, San Marcos, Poway, and 50+ more cities), Southwest Riverside County (Temecula, Murrieta, Lake Elsinore, Menifee, and more), and South Orange County (San Clemente, Mission Viejo, Laguna Niguel, Lake Forest, and more). If you're unsure, give us a call!
Prices vary based on square footage, access, condition, and scope. We offer free in-home inspections and quotes so you get an accurate price upfront — no surprises or hidden fees. We also provide bundled service discounts and seasonal promotions.
Yes. We regularly work in attics with signs of rodent activity. Our process: remove old insulation, sanitize with commercial-grade disinfectants, seal all entry points with permanent materials, install traps, and install new insulation once the attic is fully prepped. We make it safe and clean before we rebuild.
Most jobs are completed in one full day. Insulation removal and installation for a standard home takes 6–10 hours. Rodent proofing with sanitation and duct replacement may take longer depending on scope. We'll give you a detailed time estimate when we inspect your attic.

Attic Shield is specialized — we focus on attics, crawl spaces, and duct systems only. We follow the correct sequence: remove → seal → sanitize → restore. We provide major-step photo documentation, use permanent materials (never foam-only), protect your home with floor runners and barriers, and we're family-operated — not a franchise or call center.
